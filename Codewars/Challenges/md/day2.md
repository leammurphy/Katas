# Exclamation marks series #1: Remove an exclamation mark from the end of string

2022-02-07

## 8 kyu

**_Time: ~10min_**

**Remove an exclamation mark from the end of a string.**

For Example:

remove("Hi!!!") === "Hi!!"
remove("!Hi") === "!Hi"
remove("!Hi!") === "!Hi"
